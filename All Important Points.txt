30 ways to improve Your Design

1. Add more white space, let things breathe
2. Contrast is better for recognition
3. Add color to make things stand out
4. Use texture to add more of a realistic feel
5. Don't make the logo bigger
6. Use a versatile font with large families
7. Adjust kerning if letters feel weirdly spaced
8. Learn your shortcuts for faster design speed
9. Redesign famous logos and backwards engineer them
10. Break the grid using typography or shapes
11. Add drama by wrapping text or using playful fonts
12. Don't use lorem ipsum, write real words
13. Trust your gut, if it doesn't look right scrap it
14. Do more sketches, the more the better
15. Try a blending mode when mixing graphic elements
16. Pair 2 fonts that go well together, like a script and sans
17. Design for the customer not the client
18. Design a logo in black and white first
19. Photoshop is your best friend
20. Ctrl + z is your second best friend
21. Add a soft shadow to add 3D depth
22. Always customise your logo type logos
23. Get feedback from a professional designer
24. Flip it, see it in black and white, try it on a mock up
25. Use scale for emphasis or fun layouts
26. Put it next to competitors on a page or shelf to see difference
27. Less is more, unless you are going for chaotic abstract
28. Test your designs on real customers
29. Do the unexpected, people like suprises or little details
30. Play around and have fun don't copy just copy others

2. Tablets
    min-device-width => 481px and max-device-width => 768px

3. iPads
    min-device-width => 769px and max-device-width => 1024px

4. iPad Pro, Tablets Pro (Big Screens)
    min-device-width => 1025px and max-device-width => 1366px

5. Laptops, Mini Monitors
    min-device-width => 1367px and max-device-width => 1760px

6. Large Screens
